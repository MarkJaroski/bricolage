This is your home base. Here you will store all the assets you create before checking them in to a Desk. You'll also be able to check assets out from other Desks to edit them at your Workspace.

Click My Workspace in the upper left hand corner to return to this screen at any time.

Assets

You can manage three different types of assets in your Workspace: Media, Stories, and Templates. Newly created assets will reside in your Workspace until you move them -- or check them in -- to a Desk.

By checking an asset into a Desk, you allow other permitted users to access it. Desks are like stations in an assembly line - an asset needs to be approved by each one before it is ready to ship out to consumers.

For example, let's say you have created a new article for your Website. You check it in to the Edit Desk, where an editor examines its structure and accuracy. The editor then checks the article in to the Legal Desk, where a lawyer makes sure it will not get you sued. Next it goes to the Copy Desk, to be scrutinized for grammar and spelling. And finally, it is checked in to the Publish Desk, where you can push it to the site.

At each of these points, the person who needs to edit the asset will check it out, or move it to his or her Workspace. When an asset on a Desk is checked out to a user, it remains listed on that Desk and other users can still view it, but only the person who has checked it out can edit it.

Asset Properties (on Workspace)

Title
Click to preview the asset in another window (except for Templates). A white "P" in a red circle at the far end of the title field indicates that the asset has been published (or deployed, in the case of a template).
Category
Where on the site an asset is published.
Cover Date
The date that will be posted with the Story. (this is not necessarily the publish date, but what appears to viewers to be the publish date).
Priority
Tells users how urgent the asset is.
Check in to
Moves the asset to the listed Desk.
Check in to
Select the desired Desk from this select list, scroll to the bottom of the page and click Check In Assets. You can check in more than one asset at a time.
Notes
View any editorial notes users have attached to the asset.
Trail
Lists what Desks the asset has been checked in to.
Edit
Modify the asset or its profile.
Log
Displays the asset's history of actions.
Delete
Check the Delete box, scroll to the bottom of the page and click Delete Checked. You can delete more than one asset at a time.
