With this option you can search Bricolage for existing Media.

Simple Search
Enter a title, URL or keyword and click Search. For a complete list of Media, leave the space blank and click Search.
Advanced Search
This search allows you to be more specific about the Media you're trying to find. Fill in any or all of the fields and click Search. To restart your search with a fresh page, click Clear Values.
Media Found
Click View next to the desired Media to view its profile. If the Media is not checked out to another user, you can fill in the Checkout box and click the Checkout button at the bottom of the page to move it to your Workspace. If you have already checked it out, click Edit to access its profile.
Create a New Media
This link will get you to the New Media page.
